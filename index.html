RewardSplash Platform Has Closed

Thank you to all the small businesses and shoppers who have connected and grown together through the RewardSplash platform.

Note: RewardSplash was the platform provider only. For any questions about your specific rewards or accounts, please contact your business directly.

For information about what’s next, please reach out to the business owner or the store where you participated in a reward program.
